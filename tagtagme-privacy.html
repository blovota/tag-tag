Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TagTagMe ("we", "us", or "our") operates the TagTagMe platform available at tagtagme.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use TagTagMe, we may collect the following information:

  • Nickname or first name — used to identify you to other users going to the same event
  • General area — your neighbourhood or local government area (not your exact address)
  • State — the Nigerian state you are attending events from
  • WhatsApp number — collected when you say you are going to an event or posting a hangout. This is never shown publicly. It is only shared with another user when both of you have mutually accepted a connection.
  • Optional photo — if you upload a photo when saying you are going to an event, it is used to generate your shareable attending card.
  • Event information — name, location, date, type, description when you submit an event listing.
  • Hangout information — description, category, area, date when you post a hangout.
  • Connection and interest activity — tag requests you send or receive and their status (pending, accepted, declined).
  • Post-event feedback — optional responses about whether you connected with someone at an event.
  • Device and analytics data — we use Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about how users interact with TagTagMe, including pages visited, time spent, and general location (city/region). This data does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display who is going to events so others can find and connect with you
  • To facilitate connections between users attending the same event
  • To reveal your WhatsApp number only to users you have mutually accepted a connection with
  • To generate your shareable attending card image
  • To improve the TagTagMe platform based on usage patterns
  • To send you event-related reminders if you have opted in

3. Who Can See Your Information

Publicly visible: Your nickname, general area, and optional note when you say you are going to an event. Your hangout description, category, area and state when you post a hangout.

Only shared on mutual connection: Your WhatsApp number. This is never shown to any user unless both parties have accepted a connection request.

Never shared publicly: Your WhatsApp number, your exact location, or any personally identifiable information beyond what you choose to enter as your nickname.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a PostgreSQL database service. All data is stored on servers within the European Union (Ireland region). Supabase complies with industry-standard security practices including encryption at rest and in transit.

5. Data Retention

  • Attendee records for past events are automatically removed 7 days after the event date
  • Hangout posts marked as "sorted" are removed from the public feed immediately
  • Connection records are retained for up to 90 days to allow both parties to reference the connection
  • Event listings expire automatically after their event date passes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us on WhatsApp
  • Request a copy of all data we hold about your WhatsApp number
  • Withdraw from an event listing or hangout post at any time

7. Third Party Services

  • Google Analytics — we use Google Analytics to understand how TagTagMe is used. Google may collect anonymised usage data. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Meta Pixel — we may use Meta's advertising pixel to measure the effectiveness of our marketing. This collects anonymised data about visits to TagTagMe. See Meta's privacy policy at facebook.com/privacy/policy.
  • WhatsApp — connections made through TagTagMe continue on WhatsApp, which is governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy. We are not responsible for communications that occur on WhatsApp after a connection is made.
  • Bunny.net / Cloudflare — we use these services to host and deliver TagTagMe. Standard server logs may be collected by these providers.

8. Children

TagTagMe is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of TagTagMe after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, data deletion requests, or questions about this policy, please contact us via the TagTagMe platform or email us at hello@tagtagme.com.
